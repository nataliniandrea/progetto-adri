My Experiences


My name is Adriana Cancrini and I have graduated in Biomedical Engineering at Politecnico di Milano on 28th April 2021; in particular I attended the Biomechanics and Biomaterials course. In these years I have always tried to carry on my passion for programming.
I enrolled in courses of different programming languages, learning independently and achieving good results. During my entire course of study I decided to work as a waitress in my free time; this allowed me to rent an apartment, becoming a little more independent. Starting a job and university together was not so easy and there were difficult moments, but I learned how to organize myself and I started to weight time. I love traveling, I have visited Shanghai, Beijing, Jordan and Morocco in recent years; I am very curious and fascinated by different cultures.
I really want to get involved and grow professionally, I love challenges and always setting new goals. I firmly believe that working as a team is the best way to improve, I am convinced that constructive discussion and collaboration are the key to success.
